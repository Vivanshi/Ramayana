Uttara Kanda is the seventh book of the Valmiki Ramayana, which is one of the two great epics of India. In Uttara Kanda you will find the details of the exile of Sita, the birth of Luva and Kusha, their battle with Rama to catch hold the horse for Ashvamedha Yajna, Sita's final refuge in the arms of Mother Earth, and Rama's ascent to Heaven.
